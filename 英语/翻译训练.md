# 卢沟桥翻译精讲笔记 (2025-11-10 练习)

这是一份为你定制的复习笔记，按照“原文 -> 你的翻译 -> 参考答案 -> 笔记”的结构重新整理，并包含了详细的词汇、句式补充。

### 第1句

**中文原文：** 卢沟桥位于天安门广场西南15公里处，横跨永定河，是北京现存最古老的多拱石桥。

**你的翻译：** Lu Gou Bridge, located in 15 kilometers far from the east-south of the 天安门广场 ,crossing the Yong Ding River, is the oldest multi-arch stone bridge that still remains today in Beijing.

**参考答案：** Located 15 km southwest of Tian'anmen Square, Lugou Bridge stretches over the Yongding River and is the oldest existing multi-arched stone bridge in Beijing.

**笔记：**

1. **核心句式 (A, B, C)：**
   - **分析：** 这是你笔记中提到的最重要句式！中文原文包含三个并列的描述：[A: 位于...]，[B: 横跨...]，[C: 是...]。
   - **处理：** 你的方法 `A (分词), B and C` 在这里是**最佳实践**。
     - `Located...` (分词短语作状语，表示背景)
     - `Lugou Bridge` (主语)
     - `stretches...` (谓语B) `and is...` (谓语C)
   - **优点：** 句子层次分明，主次清晰，非常地道。
2. **词汇与表达：**
   - **专有名词 (拼音)：**
     - `卢沟桥` -> **Lugou Bridge**
     - `永定河` -> **Yongding River**
     - `天安门广场` -> **Tian'anmen Square**
     - **注意：**标准做法是**每个汉字拼音的首字母都大写** (例如 `Yongding`)。`Tian'anmen` 是一个特例，它是约定俗成的固定写法。
   - **横跨 (v.)：**
     - `stretch over` (强调“伸展”过...)
     - `span` (非常书面且准确的词)
     - `cross` (最简单的词)
   - **位于 (v.)：**
     - `Located...` (分词作状语)
     - `Situated...` 
     - `(which) lies...` 
   - **现存的 (adj.)：**
     - `existing` 
     - `remaining`
     - `that still remains` / `that still stands today`

### 第2句

**中文原文：** 卢沟桥最初建成于1192年，1698年重建，由281根柱子支撑。

**你的翻译：** Lu Gou Bridge was initially built in 1192 and rebuilt in 1698, with 281 pillars support it.

**参考答案：** The original construction of the bridge was completed in 1192 and then in 1698 the bridge was reconstructed. The bridge is supported by 281 pillars...

**笔记：**

1. **核心句式 (A, B, C)：**
   - **分析：** 这句又是 "A, B, C" 结构：[A: 建成]，[B: 重建]，[C: 被支撑]。
   - **处理 (方法1 - 拆分句子)：** 参考答案将 "C" 拆分成了独立的第二句 `The bridge is supported...`。
     - **优点：** 句子短小，结构清晰，绝对不会错。
   - **处理 (方法2 - 完全并列)：** 也可以用 `and` 一次性连完。
     - `Lugou Bridge was initially built in 1192, rebuilt in 1698, and is supported by 281 pillars.` (注意：`is supported` 用一般现在时，表示一个“客观事实”；前两者用过去时，表示“历史动作”)。
2. **词汇与表达：**
   - **最初建成 (v.)：**
     - `was initially built`
     - `was originally constructed`
     - `The original construction was completed...` (名词作主语)
   - **重建 (v.)：**
     - `rebuilt` (动词 `build` 的过去分词)
     - `reconstructed` (动词 `construct` 的过去分词)
   - **支撑 (v.)：**
     - `is supported by` (被动)
     - `rests on` (主动，"坐落于...")
     - `with 281 pillars supporting it` (独立主格，用在你第1句的末尾)

### 第3句

**中文原文：** 每根柱子上都有一头石狮。这些石狮的头、背、腹部或爪子上都藏着更多的狮子。

**你的翻译：** Each pillars have a stone lion on it, where its’ head, back, 腹部 or claws are underlies more lions.

**参考答案：** ...and on each pillar stands a stone lion. More lions hide themselves on the head and back, under the belly or on the paws of each lion.

**笔记：**

1. **核心句式 (倒装)：**
   - **分析：** `on each pillar stands a stone lion.` 这是一个**完全倒装句**。
   - **原因：** 英语中 "地点状语" (on each pillar) 提到句首时，谓语动词 (stands) 经常会跑到主语 (a stone lion) 前面，用以强调地点。
   - **对比 (不倒装)：** `a stone lion stands on each pillar.` (完全正确，但比较平淡)。
2. **语法细节：**
   - `Each pillar...` (主谓一致)：`Each` 后面永远跟**单数**名词 `pillar` 和单数动词 `has`。
   - `its` vs `its'` vs `it's`：`its` (它的)；`it's` (它是)；`its'` (错误拼写)。
3. **词汇与表达：**
   - **藏着 (v.)：**
     - `hide themselves` (主动)
     - `are hidden` (被动)
     - `are concealed` (被动，更书面)
   - **腹部 (n.)：** `belly` (参考答案用词), `underside`, `stomach`
   - **爪子 (n.)：** `paws`

### 第4句

**中文原文：** 这些石狮生动逼真、千姿百态，是卢沟桥石刻艺术的精品。

**你的翻译：** These stone lions are vivid and each poses differently, which are masterpiece of Lu Gou Bridge art.

**参考答案：** The lions are vivid with various postures and different expressions, and they are known as the fine works of stone carving art.

**笔记：**

1. **核心句式 (The lions...)：**
   - **语法：** 这里用 `The lions` (复数)，是因为它**特指**“上文提到的那些卢沟桥上的狮子”，而不是泛指“狮子”这个物种。这是定冠词 `the` 的“上文特指”用法。
2. **词汇与表达：**
   - **生动逼真 (adj.)：**
     - `vivid` (参考答案用词)
     - `lifelike` (最贴切“逼真”的词)
     - `realistic`
   - **千姿百态 (adj. / phrase)：**
     - `with various postures` (有不同姿态)
     - `in different poses`
     - `each poses differently` (你翻译的，很好)
   - **精品 (n.)：**
     - `fine works` (参考答案用词)
     - `masterpieces` (杰作，单数 `masterpiece`)
   - **石刻艺术 (n.)：**
     - `stone carving art` (参考答案用词)
     - `stone sculpture art`

### 第5K句

**中文原文：** 桥上的石狮不计其数，因而北京地区流传着“卢沟桥上的石狮子—“数不清”的说法。

**你的翻译：** There are countless stone lions on the bridge, so it is said popularly in Beijing area that one can’t count stone lions on Lu Gou Bridge.

**参考答案：** As there are innumerable stone lions on the bridge, there is a saying in Beijing echoing, "The lions on the Lugou Bridge are too numerous to count."

**笔记：**

1. **词汇与表达：**
   - **不计其数 / 数不清 (adj.)：**
     - `innumerable` (in + number + able)
     - `countless` (count + less)
     - `too numerous to count` (固定句式 `too...to...` 太...以至于不能...)
     - `myriad` (非常书面的词)
   - **流传着...说法 (phrase)：**
     - `there is a saying...` (最常用)
     - `a saying goes...`
     - `it is said...` (人们说...)

### 第6句

**中文原文：** 卢沟桥不仅以其美学特征闻名于世，还被公认为石桥建筑史上的一座丰碑。

**你的翻译：** Lu Gou Bridge is not only known by its artificial characteristics but also regarded as a feat in stone bridge architecture history.

**参考答案：** Lugou Bridge is not only well-known in the world for its aesthetic features but also well recognized as a monument in the architectural history of stone bridges.

**笔记：**

1. **核心句式 (Not only... but also)：**
   - `...is not only known... but also recognized...` (连接两个并列的谓语动词)
2. **词汇与表达：**
   - **闻名于世 (adj. / phrase)：**
     - `well-known for...` (固定搭配，**因**...而闻名)
     - `famous for...`
     - `renowned for...` (更书面)
   - **美学特征 (n.)：**
     - `aesthetic features` (aesthetic /esˈθetɪk/ 美学的)
     - `artistic characteristics` (艺术特色)
     - `beauty` (最简单的词)
   - **公认为 (v.)：**
     - `recognized as...`
     - `regarded as...`
     - `considered (as)...`
     - `hailed as...` (被誉为...)
   - **丰碑 (n.)：**
     - `a monument` (一座丰碑 / 纪念碑)
     - `a milestone` (一座里程碑)
     - `a feat` (一项伟绩 / 壮举)
   - **石桥建筑史 (n.)：**
     - `the architectural history of stone bridges` (石桥的建筑史)
     - `stone bridge architecture history` (你翻译的，更接近中文语序)
